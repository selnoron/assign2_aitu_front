ELON MUSK

Innovator • Visionary • Entrepreneur

About Elon Musk

Elon Musk (1971) is a technology entrepreneur, inventor, and CEO of multiple companies. He is best known for founding SpaceX, Tesla Motors, and co-founding PayPal. Musk has made a significant impact on space exploration, electric vehicles, and renewable energy.

Important Life Events

  1. Born in Pretoria, South Africa (1971)
  2. Moved to the USA and co-founded Zip2 (1996)
  3. Co-founded PayPal (1999)
  4. Founded SpaceX (2002)
  5. Became CEO of Tesla Motors (2008)
  6. Launched Starship project (2018–present)

Main Contributions

  • Reusable rockets (SpaceX)
  • Mass-market electric cars (Tesla)
  • Solar energy solutions (SolarCity)
  • Neural technology (Neuralink)
